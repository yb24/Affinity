Main Aim

The driving factor behind this project is the lack of a proper general purpose application that serves the needs of the associated stakeholders. Even today, the process of fostering and adoption is cumbersome and time consuming. With our application, we not only aim to save time for our stakeholders but also remove the social stigma of fostering and adoption from society by creating an application that is welcoming and accessible to all. We also aim to contribute in building a system that is not bounded by distance. We imagine a world where a potential parent can foster and adopt a child regardless of their location.

Key Features

We are one of the first ones to step in this domain and hence, our application is one-of-its-kind that caters to all the relevant needs of the concerned stakeholders. Contrary to contemporary adoption and fostering applications and websites, we believe in minimalism. We understand that at times, “Less is more” and hence, categorise information in a very specific manner and only provide the user with the information that is relevant to them. One of the key features of our application is the fact that we remove the prevalent barrier of distance and the medical conditions of the children in the fostering and adoption system. With the use of our application, you can connect with any foster and adoption home present anywhere in India. We empathise with the user’s needs and are sensitive towards the privacy of our user’s data. We understand our responsibilities and make sure to display the least possible but the most relevant information of the children to the potential partners. We have also included a feature to make anonymous donations. This way you can do your bit towards bringing a smile to a child’s face. We have created a system of investigation. Every potential parent has to create an account on our application. The information entered is verified from the government’s database and only after complete verification, the parent can access information of the children. This adds a layer of security and makes sure that only authentic profiles can access sensitive data.
